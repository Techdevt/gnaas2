Parse is Speaking at do {iOS}

Parse’s very own Nikita Lutsenko is headed to Amsterdam to speak on our Parse SDKs. Don’t miss his afternoon talk covering the key principles we used when building the Parse SDK for iOS and OS X. Nikita will dive deep on asynchronous programming with promises in Swift/Objective C as well as the decoupled architecture model used in the Parse SDKs.
