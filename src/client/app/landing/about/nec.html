The NEC is the supreme governing body of the Association and consists of the:

  • Chairman (The Ghana Union Conference Chaplaincy Director)
  • Vice-Chairman (The hosting Conferenceâ€™s Chaplaincy Director)
  • Secretary (The National GNAAS President)
  • Treasurer
  • National Executives
  • Field Chaplaincy Directors
  • Zonal Presidents
  • Zonal Co-ordinating Secretaries
  • Local/Institutional Presidents
  • Chapter /District Presidents, Invited Associate Members (Non-voting)
  • External and Internal Auditor
  • Pastors of Institutional GNAAS Branches
  • General Manager of SDA Schools.

The NEC has the following functions:

  • Overseeing all the activities of GNAAS.
  • Seeing to the rotation of the National Secretariat of the Association.
  • Seeing to the Regional rotation of the GNAAS National Congress.
  • Delegating duties as and when necessary.

Chaplain

The Chaplain of GNAAS is the Chaplaincy Director of the Ghana Union Conference Meetings.

The meetings of GNAAS include:

  • Zonal and Chapter Day of Fellowship is a yearly event in which GNAAS members in various zones and chapters worship together.
  • Inter-tertiary Rally is an annual event where all students in the Northern and Southern parts of Ghana meet to revitalize their spiritual life and meet fellow.
  • GNAAS members in other institutions. It is held during the Easter break each year.
  • GNAAS Congress is a one-week retreat where all GNAAS members all over the country meet to search and study the Bible and other important issues.
